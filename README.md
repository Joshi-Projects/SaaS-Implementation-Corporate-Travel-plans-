# SaaS Implementation Portfolio — Corporate Travel Platform Onboarding ✈️💼

## Project Overview 🚀

This project simulates the end-to-end implementation of a corporate travel booking SaaS platform for a fictional enterprise client, **ABC GmbH**.

The goal is to demonstrate practical understanding of SaaS onboarding, client requirements gathering, product configuration, cross-functional coordination, testing, customer training, go-live readiness, and handover to Account Management.

## Business Problem ⚠️

ABC GmbH manages business travel through a manual and decentralized process. Employees book flights and hotels using different websites, approvals are handled through email, and Finance receives travel cost information from multiple sources.

This creates operational issues such as:

* Limited visibility into travel spending
* Delayed approval workflows
* Inconsistent travel policy compliance
* Manual invoice and reporting follow-up
* No centralized view of bookings by department or cost center
* Poor user experience for employees and managers

## Implementation Goal 🎯

Implement a centralized corporate travel booking platform for **500 employees across Germany and Spain** within a simulated **6-week onboarding timeline**.

The platform should support:

* Employee access and user roles
* Travel policy configuration
* Approval workflows
* Cost center reporting
* SSO and HR data import requirements
* Customer training
* Go-live readiness
* Handover to Account Management
