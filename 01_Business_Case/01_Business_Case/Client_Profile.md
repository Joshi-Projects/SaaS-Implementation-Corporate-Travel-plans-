# Client Profile 🏢

## Client Name

ABC GmbH

## Company Type

Mid-sized technology consulting company

## Locations

* Germany
* Spain

## Number of Employees

500 employees

## Current Travel Process

ABC GmbH currently manages business travel through a manual process. Employees book flights and hotels through different websites, managers approve travel requests by email, and Finance collects travel cost information from multiple sources.

## Main Business Need

ABC GmbH wants to implement a centralized corporate travel booking SaaS platform to improve travel policy compliance, approval visibility, cost center reporting, and employee booking experience.

## Expected Benefits

* Centralized travel booking process
* Clear approval workflow
* Improved travel spend visibility
* Better cost center reporting
* Reduced manual follow-up
* Improved employee user experience
* Structured handover to Account Management after go-live
