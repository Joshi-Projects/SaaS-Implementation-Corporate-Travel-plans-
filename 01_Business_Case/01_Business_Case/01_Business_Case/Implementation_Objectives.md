# Implementation Objectives 🎯

## Project Goal

Implement a centralized corporate travel booking SaaS platform for ABC GmbH to improve travel booking efficiency, approval visibility, policy compliance, and travel spend reporting.

## Implementation Scope

The implementation will cover:

* User access setup for 500 employees
* Travel policy configuration
* Approval workflow setup
* Cost center and department reporting requirements
* SSO login requirement documentation
* HR employee data import requirements
* Finance reporting requirements
* Customer training plan
* Go-live readiness checklist
* Handover to Account Management

## Business Objectives

### 1. Centralize Travel Booking

Move employees from multiple external booking websites to one structured corporate travel booking platform.

### 2. Improve Approval Visibility

Create clear approval rules for bookings that exceed company travel limits.

### 3. Support Travel Cost Control

Apply travel policy limits for hotels and flights to support better spending control.

### 4. Improve Reporting Accuracy

Enable Finance and department leaders to view travel spend by department and cost center.

### 5. Reduce Manual Follow-Up

Reduce manual email-based approval tracking and fragmented invoice follow-up.

### 6. Improve User Experience

Give employees, managers, and admins a clearer and more consistent booking process.

### 7. Prepare for Successful Go-Live

Ensure requirements, configuration, testing, training, and handover are completed before launch.

## Success Definition

The implementation is considered successful when:

* Required user roles are defined
* Travel policy rules are documented
* Approval workflow is validated
* Integration requirements are captured
* Training materials are prepared
* Go-live checklist is completed
* Handover document is ready for Account Management
