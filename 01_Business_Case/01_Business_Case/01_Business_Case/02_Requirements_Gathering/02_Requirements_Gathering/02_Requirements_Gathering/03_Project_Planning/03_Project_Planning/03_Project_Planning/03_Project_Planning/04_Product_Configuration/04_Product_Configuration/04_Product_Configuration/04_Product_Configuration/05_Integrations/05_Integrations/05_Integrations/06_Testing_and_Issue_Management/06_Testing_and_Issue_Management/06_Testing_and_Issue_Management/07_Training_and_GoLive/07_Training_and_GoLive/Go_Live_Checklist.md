# Go-Live Checklist 🚀

## Purpose

This checklist confirms that ABC GmbH is ready to launch the corporate travel booking SaaS platform.

The goal is to ensure that requirements, configuration, testing, training, access, reporting, and support readiness are completed before employees start using the platform.

## Go-Live Readiness Areas

The go-live decision should review:

* Requirements confirmation
* Travel policy configuration
* Approval workflow setup
* User roles and access
* SSO and data readiness
* Reporting requirements
* UAT testing results
* Open issues and blockers
* Customer training completion
* Support and handover readiness

## Go-Live Decision Criteria

Go-live can proceed when:

* High-priority requirements are confirmed
* Critical configuration items are documented and tested
* User access requirements are ready
* Approval workflow has been validated
* Reporting needs are reviewed
* Training materials have been shared
* Critical UAT issues are resolved
* Support process is confirmed
* Client stakeholders approve launch readiness

## Go-Live Risk Notes

Go-live should be delayed or reviewed if:

* SSO login is not ready and no fallback exists
* Approval workflow is not working
* Employee data is incomplete
* Finance reporting requirements are unclear
* Critical issues remain unresolved
* Customer training has not been completed

## Business Value

A structured go-live checklist helps reduce launch risk, improve customer confidence, and ensure that the implementation moves from setup to adoption in a controlled and professional way.
