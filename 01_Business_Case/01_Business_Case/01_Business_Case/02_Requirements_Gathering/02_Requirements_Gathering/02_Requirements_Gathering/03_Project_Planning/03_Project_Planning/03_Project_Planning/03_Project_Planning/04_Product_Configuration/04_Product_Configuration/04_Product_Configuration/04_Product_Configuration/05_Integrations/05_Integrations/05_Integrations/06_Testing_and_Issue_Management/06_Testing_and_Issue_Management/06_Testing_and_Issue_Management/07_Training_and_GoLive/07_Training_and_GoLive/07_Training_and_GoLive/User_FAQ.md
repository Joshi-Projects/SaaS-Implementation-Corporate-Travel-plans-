# User FAQ 📘

## Purpose

This FAQ provides basic guidance for ABC GmbH employees, managers, Finance users, and admins before and after go-live of the corporate travel booking platform.

The goal is to reduce confusion, support user adoption, and answer common questions about login, booking, approvals, travel policy, and support.

## General Questions

### What is the new corporate travel booking platform?

The corporate travel booking platform is a centralized system where ABC GmbH employees can book business travel, including flights, hotels, and rental cars, while following company travel policies.

### Why is ABC GmbH using this platform?

ABC GmbH is using the platform to centralize business travel bookings, improve approval visibility, support travel cost control, and provide better reporting for Finance and leadership.

### Who can use the platform?

Employees, department managers, travel administrators, Finance users, HR users, and selected senior leaders can use the platform based on their assigned role.

## Login and Access

### How do employees log in?

Employees are expected to log in using their company Microsoft account through Single Sign-On, if SSO is ready before go-live.

### What should I do if I cannot log in?

Employees should first check that they are using their company email address. If the issue continues, they should contact the travel administrator or support contact shared during go-live.

### Can external contractors access the platform?

Contractor access is still to be confirmed and depends on ABC GmbH’s final access policy.

## Booking Travel

### What can employees book?

Employees can book business travel such as flights, hotels, and rental cars, depending on company policy and platform availability.

### Do I need approval for every booking?

No. Bookings within company policy can be auto-approved. Bookings above policy limits may require manager approval.

### What happens if I book a hotel above the company limit?

If the hotel price is above the country-specific limit, the booking is flagged as out-of-policy and sent to the department manager for approval.

### What are the hotel limits?

* Germany: €400 per night
* Spain: €350 per night

### What happens if my flight costs more than €500?

Flights above €500 require manager approval before confirmation.

### Can I book business class?

Business class requires senior leadership approval.

## Approval Workflow

### Who approves out-of-policy bookings?

Department managers approve most out-of-policy bookings. Senior leaders approve exceptional bookings such as business class travel.

### What happens if my manager does not approve in time?

If approval is delayed beyond 48 hours, the request may be escalated according to the approval escalation process.

### How will I know if my booking is approved?

The platform should show booking status updates, including whether the booking is approved, pending approval, or rejected.

## Cost Centers and Reporting

### Why do I need to enter a cost center?

Cost center information helps Finance track travel spending by department and allocate costs correctly.

### What booking information is used for reporting?

Reporting may include employee name, department, cost center, country, booking type, booking value, approval status, and policy status.

## Support

### Who should I contact for help?

Employees should contact the travel administrator or the support contact shared during go-live.

### What should I include when reporting an issue?

Include:

* Your name
* Company email
* Booking type
* Screenshot or error message if available
* Description of the issue
* When the issue happened

## Go-Live Support

### What happens during the first week after go-live?

During the first week, the implementation and support teams monitor login issues, booking attempts, approval problems, and reporting-related questions.

### Will training material be available after go-live?

Yes. Employees should receive an FAQ or quick-start guide, and training materials may be shared as a recording or document.

## FAQ Maintenance

This FAQ should be updated after training sessions, UAT testing, and the first week of go-live support based on user questions and recurring issues.
