# Timeline and Milestones 📅

## Purpose

This document outlines the simulated 6-week implementation timeline for onboarding ABC GmbH to the corporate travel booking SaaS platform.

The timeline helps create visibility across key phases, milestones, deliverables, dependencies, and decision points.

## Implementation Timeline Overview

| Week   | Phase                                | Key Activities                                                                                    | Main Deliverables                                       |
| ------ | ------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| Week 1 | Kick-Off and Discovery               | Confirm scope, stakeholders, goals, current process, and communication rhythm                     | Discovery notes, stakeholder list, initial requirements |
| Week 2 | Requirements Validation              | Validate travel policy, approval workflow, access, reporting, and integration needs               | Requirements checklist, validation log, open questions  |
| Week 3 | Configuration and Integration Review | Document travel policy setup, approval rules, user roles, cost centers, SSO and data import needs | Configuration documents, integration requirements       |
| Week 4 | Testing and Issue Management         | Test login, user roles, booking flow, approval workflow, reporting, and issue tracking            | UAT checklist, issue log, blocker escalation process    |
| Week 5 | Training and Go-Live Preparation     | Prepare admin, manager, and employee training materials; confirm readiness items                  | Training plan, FAQ, go-live checklist draft             |
| Week 6 | Go-Live and Handover                 | Launch platform, monitor first bookings, resolve critical issues, hand over to Account Manager    | Go-live checklist, handover document, success metrics   |

## Key Milestones

### Milestone 1: Project Kick-Off Completed

**Target:** End of Week 1
**Success Criteria:**

* Project scope confirmed
* Key stakeholders identified
* Communication cadence agreed
* Initial requirements captured

### Milestone 2: Requirements Validated

**Target:** End of Week 2
**Success Criteria:**

* Travel policy rules confirmed
* Approval workflow confirmed
* User role requirements confirmed
* Reporting requirements reviewed
* Technical requirements moved to review

### Milestone 3: Configuration Requirements Ready

**Target:** End of Week 3
**Success Criteria:**

* Travel policy configuration documented
* Approval workflow documented
* User roles and access documented
* Cost center reporting requirements documented
* Integration requirements captured

### Milestone 4: UAT Testing Completed

**Target:** End of Week 4
**Success Criteria:**

* Login and access tested
* Travel policy rules tested
* Approval workflow tested
* Reporting requirements tested
* Critical issues logged and assigned

### Milestone 5: Training Completed

**Target:** End of Week 5
**Success Criteria:**

* Admin training prepared
* Manager training prepared
* Employee FAQ prepared
* Training attendance confirmed
* Open training questions documented

### Milestone 6: Go-Live Approved

**Target:** Week 6
**Success Criteria:**

* Go-live checklist completed
* Critical issues resolved
* Client sign-off received
* First-week support process confirmed

### Milestone 7: Handover Completed

**Target:** End of Week 6
**Success Criteria:**

* Client setup summary documented
* Key contacts confirmed
* Open items listed
* Success metrics defined
* Account Manager handover completed

## Dependencies

| Dependency                       | Impact if Delayed        | Owner                  |
| -------------------------------- | ------------------------ | ---------------------- |
| Final travel policy approval     | Delays configuration     | Finance Manager        |
| Employee data file               | Delays user access setup | HR Manager             |
| SSO technical review             | Delays login testing     | IT Contact             |
| Approval workflow confirmation   | Delays UAT testing       | Operations Lead        |
| Training attendance confirmation | Delays go-live readiness | Implementation Manager |
| Client sign-off                  | Delays launch            | Project Sponsor        |

## Timeline Management Notes

* Weekly status meetings should review milestones, open risks, and blockers.
* Any delayed dependency should be escalated with clear options and revised timelines.
* Go-live should not proceed until critical requirements, access, testing, and training readiness are confirmed.
