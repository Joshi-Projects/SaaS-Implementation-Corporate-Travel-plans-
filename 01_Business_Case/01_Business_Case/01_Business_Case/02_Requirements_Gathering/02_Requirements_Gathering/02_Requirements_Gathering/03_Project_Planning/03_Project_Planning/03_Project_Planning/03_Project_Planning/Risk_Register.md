# Risk Register 🚨

## Purpose

This risk register tracks potential risks that could impact the successful implementation of the corporate travel booking SaaS platform for ABC GmbH.

The goal is to identify risks early, assign ownership, define mitigation actions, and prevent delays before go-live.

## Risk Severity Guide

### High Impact

Could delay go-live, block configuration, or affect customer adoption.

### Medium Impact

Could create rework, confusion, or additional follow-up.

### Low Impact

Can be managed without major impact on timeline or launch readiness.

## Risk Management Approach

* Review risks weekly during implementation status meetings.
* Assign one clear owner for each risk.
* Escalate high-impact risks early with options and recommended next steps.
* Update risk status when mitigation actions are completed.
* Do not proceed to go-live while high-priority risks remain unresolved.

## Implementation Value

A structured risk register helps the Implementation Manager move from reactive problem-solving to proactive project control, improving timeline reliability, stakeholder visibility, and go-live readiness.
