# Customer Training Plan 🎓

## Purpose

This document outlines the customer training plan for ABC GmbH before go-live of the corporate travel booking SaaS platform.

The goal is to ensure that admins, managers, Finance users, and employees understand how to use the platform, follow travel policy rules, manage approvals, and know where to get support after launch.

## Training Objective

Prepare ABC GmbH users for successful adoption of the travel booking platform by providing role-specific training and practical guidance before go-live.

## Training Audience

| User Group            | Training Focus                                                | Format                | Priority |
| --------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------- | -------- |
| Travel Administrators | Platform basics, user support, policy overview                | Live session          | High     |
| Department Managers   | Approval workflow, out-of-policy bookings, escalation process | Live session          | High     |
| Finance Users         | Reporting, cost centers, travel spend visibility              | Live session          | High     |
| Employees             | Booking flow, travel policy, approval status, FAQ             | Guide / Recorded demo | Medium   |
| HR Users              | Employee data, user access, manager mapping                   | Live session          | Medium   |

## Training Topics

### 1. Admin Training

Admin users should understand:

* Platform overview
* User roles and access
* Travel policy setup overview
* Common employee questions
* How to escalate issues after go-live

### 2. Manager Training

Managers should understand:

* How approval requests are triggered
* How to approve or reject bookings
* How to review booking value and policy limit
* What happens if approval is delayed
* How escalation works

### 3. Finance Training

Finance users should understand:

* Cost center reporting
* Department-level travel spend visibility
* Out-of-policy booking reports
* Required booking data fields
* Monthly reporting expectations

### 4. Employee Training

Employees should understand:

* How to log in
* How to search and book travel
* How to identify in-policy and out-of-policy bookings
* How approval requests work
* Where to find help after go-live

## Training Materials

* Admin training agenda
* Manager approval workflow guide
* Employee quick-start guide
* User FAQ
* Recorded training session
* Go-live support contact information

## Training Timeline

| Week   | Activity                                   | Owner                               |
| ------ | ------------------------------------------ | ----------------------------------- |
| Week 4 | Prepare training materials                 | Implementation Manager              |
| Week 5 | Deliver admin and manager training         | Implementation Manager              |
| Week 5 | Share employee FAQ and quick-start guide   | Implementation Manager / HR Manager |
| Week 6 | Confirm training completion before go-live | Implementation Manager              |

## Training Success Criteria

Training is considered complete when:

* Admin users understand basic platform support responsibilities
* Managers understand approval workflow steps
* Finance understands reporting and cost center requirements
* Employees receive quick-start guidance
* FAQ is shared before go-live
* Open training questions are documented

## Post-Training Follow-Up

After training, the Implementation Manager should:

* Collect open questions
* Update FAQ if needed
* Track unresolved training topics
* Confirm attendance
* Include training completion in go-live readiness review

## Business Value

A structured customer training plan helps ABC GmbH reduce confusion, improve user adoption, reduce support requests, and increase confidence before launch.
