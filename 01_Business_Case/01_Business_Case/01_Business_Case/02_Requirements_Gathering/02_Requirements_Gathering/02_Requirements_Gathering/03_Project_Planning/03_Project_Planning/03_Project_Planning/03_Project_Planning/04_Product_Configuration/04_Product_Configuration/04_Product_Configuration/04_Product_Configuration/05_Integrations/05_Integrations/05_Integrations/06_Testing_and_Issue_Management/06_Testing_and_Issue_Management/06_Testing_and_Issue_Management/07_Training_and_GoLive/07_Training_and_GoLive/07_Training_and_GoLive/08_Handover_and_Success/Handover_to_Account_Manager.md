# Handover to Account Manager 🤝

## Purpose

This document summarizes the implementation status of ABC GmbH and provides the Account Manager with the context needed to manage the customer relationship after go-live.

The goal is to ensure a smooth transition from implementation to long-term account management, with clear visibility into configuration decisions, key contacts, open items, risks, and next steps.

| Platform                | Corporate travel booking SaaS platform  |
| Go-Live Date            | 15 August 2026                          |
| Implementation Duration | 6 weeks                                 |

## Implementation Summary

The corporate travel booking platform was implemented to centralize ABC GmbH’s business travel process and improve travel policy compliance, approval visibility, cost center reporting, and employee booking experience.

The implementation included:

* Client requirements gathering
* Travel policy configuration documentation
* Approval workflow setup
* User roles and access definition
* SSO login requirement documentation
* HR and Finance data flow requirements
* UAT testing checklist
* Issue and blocker tracking
* Customer training plan
* Go-live checklist
* User FAQ

## Open Items for Account Manager Follow-Up

| Open Item                                         | Owner                     | Recommended Follow-Up                                    |
| ------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |

## Known Risks After Go-Live

| Risk                                                    | Potential Impact             | Suggested Action                          |
| ------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Employees may misunderstand out-of-policy booking rules | More support questions       | Share FAQ and examples again after launch |
| Finance reporting format may need refinement            | Additional reporting changes | Review report output after first month    |
| Manager approvals may be delayed                        | Booking delays               | Monitor approval delay report             |
| SSO issue may affect some users                         | Login support requests       | Keep fallback support process ready       |

## Recommended 30-Day Review Agenda

* Review platform adoption and booking activity
* Review support issues from first month
* Review out-of-policy booking trends
* Review travel spend by department and country
* Confirm Finance reporting satisfaction
* Discuss open configuration or reporting improvements
* Capture new customer requests for Product / Account Management follow-up

## Success Metrics to Track

* User adoption rate
* Number of successful bookings
* Number of approval requests
* Approval delay rate
* Out-of-policy booking rate
* Support tickets after go-live
* Finance reporting completeness
* Customer satisfaction after onboarding

## Handover Notes

The Account Manager should focus on adoption, reporting value, process refinement, and customer satisfaction during the first 30 days after go-live.

Any unresolved configuration, reporting, or integration topics should be tracked as post-go-live follow-up items.
