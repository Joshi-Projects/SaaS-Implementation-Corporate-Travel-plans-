# Implementation Project Plan 📅

## Purpose

This document outlines the simulated implementation plan for onboarding ABC GmbH to a corporate travel booking SaaS platform.

The plan is structured to guide the project from kick-off through requirements gathering, configuration, integration review, testing, training, go-live, and handover to Account Management.

## Project Duration

6 weeks

## Project Objective

Implement a centralized travel booking platform for 500 employees across Germany and Spain, enabling structured travel policies, approval workflows, cost center reporting, user access, training, and go-live readiness.

## Phase 1: Kick-Off and Discovery

**Timeline:** Week 1

### Key Activities

* Confirm project scope and business objectives
* Identify client-side and SaaS provider stakeholders
* Review current travel booking process
* Capture high-level client requirements
* Define project communication cadence
* Confirm target go-live date

### Outputs

* Client discovery notes
* Stakeholder list
* Initial requirements checklist
* Project communication plan

## Phase 2: Requirements Validation

**Timeline:** Week 1–2

### Key Activities

* Validate travel policy requirements
* Confirm approval workflow rules
* Confirm user roles and access needs
* Review finance and reporting requirements
* Review integration requirements with IT
* Identify open questions and decision owners

### Outputs

* Requirements checklist
* Requirement validation log
* Open questions list
* Initial risk register

## Phase 3: Product Configuration Planning

**Timeline:** Week 2–3

### Key Activities

* Define travel policy setup
* Document approval workflow logic
* Define user roles and access levels
* Confirm cost center and reporting structure
* Coordinate configuration inputs with relevant teams

### Outputs

* Travel policy configuration document
* Approval workflow setup document
* User roles and access document
* Cost center reporting requirements

## Phase 4: Integration Review

**Timeline:** Week 3

### Key Activities

* Review SSO login requirement
* Confirm HR employee data import needs
* Define finance reporting export expectations
* Capture technical dependencies and blockers
* Coordinate questions for technical team review

### Outputs

* Integration requirements document
* SSO requirement brief
* HR and Finance data flow document
* Technical open questions list

## Phase 5: Testing and Issue Management

**Timeline:** Week 4

### Key Activities

* Test user login and role access
* Test travel policy rules
* Test approval workflow
* Test reporting requirements
* Track issues and blockers
* Escalate high-priority issues before go-live

### Outputs

* UAT testing checklist
* Issue log
* Blocker escalation process
* Updated risk register

## Phase 6: Customer Training

**Timeline:** Week 5

### Key Activities

* Prepare admin training
* Prepare manager approval workflow training
* Prepare employee quick-start guide
* Prepare user FAQ
* Confirm training attendance
* Collect training feedback

### Outputs

* Customer training plan
* User FAQ
* Training completion summary

## Phase 7: Go-Live Readiness

**Timeline:** Week 6

### Key Activities

* Review go-live checklist
* Confirm high-priority issues are closed
* Confirm user access readiness
* Confirm client sign-off
* Monitor first bookings after launch
* Provide first-week support structure

### Outputs

* Go-live checklist
* Go-live readiness confirmation
* First-week support plan

## Phase 8: Handover to Account Management

**Timeline:** End of Week 6

### Key Activities

* Prepare client setup summary
* Document completed configuration items
* List open follow-up items
* Confirm key contacts
* Share risks, lessons learned, and next steps
* Hand over client to Account Manager for long-term relationship management

### Outputs

* Handover document
* Post-go-live support plan
* Success metrics
* Lessons learned

## Implementation Manager Responsibilities

* Maintain project structure and timelines
* Capture and validate client requirements
* Coordinate with internal and client-side stakeholders
* Track risks, issues, blockers, and dependencies
* Prepare training and go-live readiness documents
* Ensure clean handover to Account Management

## Success Criteria

The implementation is successful when:

* Requirements are validated
* Configuration requirements are documented
* Testing is completed
* Critical issues are resolved before go-live
* Customer training is completed
* Go-live checklist is approved
* Handover to Account Management is complete
