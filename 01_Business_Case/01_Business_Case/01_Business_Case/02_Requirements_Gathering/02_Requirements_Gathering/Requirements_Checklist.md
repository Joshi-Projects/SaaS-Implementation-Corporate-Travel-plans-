# Requirements Checklist ✅

## Purpose

This checklist captures the confirmed and open requirements for the corporate travel platform implementation at ABC GmbH.

It helps ensure that client needs are clearly documented, validated, assigned to owners, and tracked before configuration, testing, training, and go-live.

## Requirements Overview

| Requirement Area  | Requirement                                                                        | Priority | Owner                  | Status    |
| ----------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------- | ---------------------- | --------- |
| User Access       | Platform access for 500 employees across Germany and Spain                         | High     | HR Manager             | Confirmed |
| User Roles        | Define access levels for employees, managers, admins, and Finance users            | High     | Implementation Manager | Confirmed |
| Travel Policy     | Standard hotel limit of €400 per night for Germany                                 | High     | Finance Manager        | Confirmed |
| Travel Policy     | Standard hotel limit of €350 per night for Spain                                   | High     | Finance Manager        | Confirmed |
| Travel Policy     | Flights above €500 require manager approval                                        | High     | Finance Manager        | Confirmed |
| Travel Policy     | Business class requires senior leadership approval                                 | Medium   | Finance Manager        | Confirmed |
| Approval Workflow | Department managers approve out-of-policy bookings                                 | High     | Operations Lead        | Confirmed |
| Approval Workflow | Delayed approvals should be escalated after 48 hours                               | Medium   | Operations Lead        | Open      |
| Cost Centers      | Every booking must include department and cost center information                  | High     | Finance Manager        | Confirmed |
| Reporting         | Monthly travel spend report by department                                          | High     | Finance Manager        | Confirmed |
| Reporting         | Monthly travel spend report by country                                             | Medium   | Finance Manager        | Open      |
| Integration       | SSO login requirement using company Microsoft account                              | High     | IT Contact             | In Review |
| Integration       | HR employee data import for names, emails, departments, managers, and cost centers | High     | HR Manager             | In Review |
| Integration       | Finance reporting export for booking and cost center data                          | Medium   | Finance Manager        | Open      |
| Training          | Admin training before go-live                                                      | High     | Implementation Manager | Planned   |
| Training          | Manager approval workflow training                                                 | High     | Implementation Manager | Planned   |
| Training          | Employee FAQ and quick-start guide                                                 | Medium   | Implementation Manager | Planned   |
| Go-Live           | Final go-live readiness checklist required                                         | High     | Implementation Manager | Planned   |
| Handover          | Handover document required for Account Manager                                     | High     | Implementation Manager | Planned   |

## Requirement Status Definitions

* **Confirmed** — Requirement has been agreed with the client.
* **Open** — Requirement needs further clarification.
* **In Review** — Requirement is being reviewed by the relevant stakeholder.
* **Planned** — Requirement is accepted and will be completed in a later project phase.

## Notes

Requirements should be reviewed weekly during implementation status meetings. Any change in scope, priority, ownership, or timeline should be documented and communicated to the relevant stakeholders.
