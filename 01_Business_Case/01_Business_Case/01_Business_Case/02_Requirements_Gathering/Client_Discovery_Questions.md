# Client Discovery Questions 📝

## Purpose

This document outlines the key discovery questions used during the implementation kick-off and requirements gathering phase for ABC GmbH.

The goal is to understand the client’s current travel process, business rules, approval needs, reporting requirements, integration expectations, and training needs before configuring the corporate travel booking platform.

## 1. Company Setup

* How many employees will use the platform at go-live?
* Which countries and office locations should be included?
* Who will act as the main project sponsor?
* Who will be the daily client-side project contact?
* Who will be responsible for platform administration after go-live?

## 2. User Access

* Which employee groups need access to the platform?
* Are all 500 employees included in the initial rollout?
* Should access be different for employees, managers, admins, and Finance users?
* Who should approve new user access requests?
* Should access be connected to the company’s login system?

## 3. Travel Policy

* What is the maximum hotel budget per night?
* Should hotel limits differ by country or city?
* What is the maximum flight budget before approval is required?
* Are employees allowed to book business class?
* Are rental cars allowed for all employees or only specific roles?
* What should happen when a booking is outside company policy?

## 4. Approval Workflow

* Who approves travel bookings?
* Should approval depend on booking value?
* Should approval depend on department, country, or employee role?
* How quickly should managers approve requests?
* What should happen if approval is delayed?

## 5. Finance and Reporting

* Which cost centers should be used?
* Should every booking include department and cost center information?
* What monthly reports does Finance need?
* Should reporting show travel spend by department, country, or employee group?
* Who should receive travel spend reports?

## 6. Integrations

* Should employees log in using company SSO?
* Which login provider should be used, such as Microsoft or Google?
* Is HR employee data import required?
* Which employee data fields are required for import?
* Does Finance need booking data export for reporting or invoice follow-up?

## 7. Training and Change Management

* Which user groups need training?
* Should training be live, recorded, or both?
* Who needs admin training?
* Who needs manager approval training?
* What information should be included in the employee FAQ?

## 8. Go-Live Readiness

* What is the target go-live date?
* Are there any business-critical travel dates before launch?
* Who signs off final readiness?
* What support is needed during the first week after go-live?
* What should be included in the handover to Account Management?
