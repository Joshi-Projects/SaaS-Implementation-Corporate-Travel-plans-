# Problem Statement ⚠️

## Current Situation

ABC GmbH currently manages business travel through a manual and decentralized process. Employees book flights and hotels using different websites, managers approve trips through email, and Finance receives travel cost information from multiple sources.

This creates delays, inconsistent reporting, and limited visibility into business travel spending.

## Key Problems

* Employees use different platforms to book business travel
* Managers handle approvals manually through email
* Finance receives invoice and cost information from multiple sources
* Travel policy compliance is difficult to monitor
* Department-level travel spending is not clearly visible
* Booking data is not centralized for reporting
* Employees and managers have an inconsistent user experience

## Business Impact

The current process creates operational inefficiency, increases manual follow-up, and makes it harder for ABC GmbH to control travel costs, monitor policy compliance, and make data-driven decisions.

## Implementation Need

ABC GmbH needs a centralized corporate travel booking platform that can support structured travel policies, approval workflows, cost center reporting, user access, training, and go-live readiness.

The implementation should help ABC GmbH move from a fragmented manual process to a structured SaaS-based travel management process.
